How It Works

iuvō (Latin for 'I help!') automatically contacts people you trust if you don't make it home on time.

1. Register and Set Contacts


After signing up for the service, go to the Add Contacts page and enter the names and email addresses of some trusted friends. (Don't worry; we'll never spam them or share their contact info with anyone. Scout's honor!)

2. Flying Solo? Set Up An Alert!


Before you leave for that solo trek of the Appalachian trail, just enter when you're leaving, when you're going to come back, and a message. If you get held up and don't turn off the notification before a specific time, your message will automatically be sent to the contacts you selected.

3. Go out and have fun.


You can go on your hike or your date without any worries.
Once you're back from your event, turn off your reminder so your friends won't get an email telling them to check in with you…even though you've got lots of great stories to tell!
