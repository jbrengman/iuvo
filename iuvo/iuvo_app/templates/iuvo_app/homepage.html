How It Works

iuvo (Latin for 'I help!') gives you peace of mind when you're out and about by automatically contacting people you trust if you don't make it home by the time you expect to be.

Register & Add Contacts


After signing up for the service, go to the Add Contacts page and enter the names and email addresses of some trusted friends. (Don't worry; we'll never spam them or share their contact info with anyone. Scout's honor!)

Alerts are sent automatically.


If you don't log in and turn off the alert by the specified time, emails will be sent to your contacts to check in with you.

Go out and have fun.


You can go on your hike or your date without any worries.
Once you're back from your event, turn off your reminder so your friends won't get an email telling them to check in with you!
